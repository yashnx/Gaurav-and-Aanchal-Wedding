Wedding Itinerary

Mehendi Function

5th April 2025, 12 PM onwards

In many Sindhi weddings, the Mehendi ceremony is a vibrant affair, symbolizing joy and beauty as intricate henna designs adorn the bride’s hands. Family and friends gather to celebrate, sing songs, and get a head start on the wedding festivities.

Sangeet

5th April 2025, 7 PM onwards

The Sangeet is a night of music and dance. It’s a chance for both families to come together, break the ice, and celebrate the upcoming union. Expect fun performances, DJ, dhol, and plenty of laughter.

Laava Ceremony at Banno Sahib Gurudwara

6th April 2025, 10:30 AM

The sacred Laava (also known as the Anand Karaj in Sikh tradition) is a beautiful ceremony that unites the couple in holy matrimony. It involves four rounds around the Guru Granth Sahib, each signifying a vow and a stage of married life.

Gaurav's Janeu Ceremony

9th September 2024 marked Gaurav’s Janeu ceremony, a significant rite of passage in Hindu tradition. The Janeu (sacred thread) represents the start of a new phase of life, bearing responsibilities and upholding family values. It’s a deeply spiritual and cultural milestone that connects one to the roots of tradition.
